Ingredients

1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon turmeric
1 cup cooked chana dal
- 1/2 cup ghee or oil
1 cup grated jaggery
- Filling (puran)
1 cup grated jaggery
- Water (as needed)
1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
- 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg powder

Dough Preparation (10 minutes)1. Mix flour, salt, and turmeric. Add ghee or oil and mix.Gradually add water to form a soft dough. Filling Preparation (10 minutes) Mix cooked chana dal, jaggery, cardamom powder, and nutmeg powder.Assembly and Rolling (10 minutes) Divide dough into small portions. Roll out each portion into a circle.Place filling in the center. Fold and seal the dough. Cooking (5 minutes):Heat a non-stick pan or tava. Cook poli for 2 minutes (until golden).Flip and cook for 2 minutes.

3.Bhaji

Ingredients: - 1 cup mixed vegetables (onion, potato, cauliflower, carrot)
- 1/2 cup gram flour (besan)
- 1/4 cup water
- 1/4 teaspoon cumin seeds
- 1/4 teaspoon coriander powder
- Salt, to taste
- Oil, for frying
Short Steps: 1. Chop vegetables into small pieces. Mix gram flour, water, cumin seeds, coriander powder, salt. Add vegetables to mixture.Shape into small balls or patties. Fry until golden brown.
